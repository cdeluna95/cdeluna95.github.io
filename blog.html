    My Favorite Location

    Boracay, Philippines

    This is Boracay (boh-RAH-kai), one of the best beaches in the world. The waters are beautiful and crystal-clear, especially in the vivid sunset. For this reason, it's a perfect place for relaxation as well as family and friend gatherings. It's also great if you plan on having a wedding celebration here!
