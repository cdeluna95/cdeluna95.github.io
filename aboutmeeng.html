About Me

Background

Christan de Luna, better known as Christian (born August 20, 1995), is a Filipino junior student in Georgia Gwinnett College.

Born in Manila, Philippines, De Luna grew up in the city for 8 years, frequently traveling back and forth from the Philippines and Saudi Arabia. At the age of 8, he moved to the United States in New York in October 2003 and finally in Georgia in October 2005. He had eventually adapted to the American culture, thus ultimately gaining dual citizenship. He currently attends Georgia Gwinnett College. He currently majors in Information Technology, specifically in the Software Development concentration.

Interests

In his free time, De Luna likes to spend time on his computer. He likes to browse the Internet, talk with his friends on Skype, watch videos on YouTube, search threads in reddit, go to gaming message boards, and more. He also likes to play video games, particularly console, portable, and PC games. Sometimes, he likes to play sports such as soccer, bicycling, and swimming. Recently, he started doing fitness, like weight lifting.
