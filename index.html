About

Hi, I'm Christan! I'm a Georgia Gwinnett College graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology with a concentration in Software Development. I'm a highly driven and aspiring software developer always looking for ways to sharpen my knowledge, skills, and abilities in the field of web development and design. While my passion remains in the front-end focused on user interface and user experience design, I'd also love to go the full-stack route as well. I want to learn the best coding practices and transform them into the industry-standard, as well as give myself exposure to a professional environment. When I'm not coding, you'll see me binge-watching videos, making drawings or sketches, immersing myself into the world of video games, or working out!
